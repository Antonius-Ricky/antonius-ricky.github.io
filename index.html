Discord Gacha Bot

Introducing my Discord Gacha bot project! This unique bot adds an exciting twist to the Discord experience. Users can roll for virtual fruits inspired by the popular Blox Fruits game, and each fruit they obtain is neatly stored in their personal inventory. The interactive and engaging nature of the bot has captured the enthusiasm of all users.

Key Features:

  • Fruit Rolling: Users can roll for a variety of fruits, each with its own rarity and properties based on the Blox Fruits game.
  • Inventory System: The bot features a user-friendly inventory system that organizes and displays the collected fruits for each user.
  • Ranking System: Users can compete for the top spot on the leaderboard, showcasing their achievements and collection prowess.
  • Community Engagement: The bot has fostered a vibrant community where users eagerly participate in fruit rolling and share their unique collections.

Instagram Clone

Presenting my Instagram Clone project, a web app designed to emulate the core functionalities of Instagram. This project reflects my passion for creating dynamic and feature-rich web applications.

Key Features:

  • Photo Sharing: Users can seamlessly upload and share their favorite photos with the community.
  • Like System: The app incorporates a familiar liking mechanism, allowing users to express appreciation for photos posted by others.
  • Profile Editing: Users have the flexibility to customize and edit their profiles, adding a personal touch to their online presence.
  • Intuitive Design: The user interface is designed to mirror the aesthetic appeal and user-friendly experience of the original Instagram platform.
